K-mers

In bioinformatics, k-mers are subsequences of length k contained within a biological sequence. The presence of k-mers in genomes can account for diverse genetic events such as acquiring SNPs, (long) insertions/deletions, and recombinations. Unlike SNPs or gene-based approaches, k-mer analyses do not require a reference genome or any assumption on the nature of the causal variants and can even be performed without assembling the genome sequences
