Significant K-mer sequences for Acinetobacter baumannii

Results

For Acinetobacter baumannii, the best model was obtained when using a k-mer length of 12, where all the models reported a test and train accuracy over 0.95. Adaboost, Xgboost, DT, BC, and SVM obtained accuracies of 0.99 for the testing, and in the majority of the cases, they got training accuracies of 1.

In the case of Pseudomonas aeruginosa, best models using a k-mer length of 12 shows accuracies of 0.99 for training, where Bagging Classifier got an accuracy of 0.93 (BC 30/70), followed by the Adaboost model with an accuracy of 0.92. These results surpass results reported previously on state-on-art for carbapenem resistance in these bacteria. With this, it can be concluded that the k-mer set created for ML can classify sensible and resistant strains of these bacteria.
